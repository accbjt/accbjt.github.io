Scion Xa Speedster

This project was done at 5 Axis Models. I was part of a very talented group of desigeners, fabricators, and engineers. My primary role was as a Model Maker. My responsibility was to build, fabricate, and assemble the project. We took the Scion Xa and chopped the top, lowered the front windshield, removed the doors, build custom side panels, built custom rear panels, designed and built custom bumpers and rocker panels, and installed a custom entertainment system. The hood popped open to expose a proector screen where you can play Grand Turismo using a custom steering wheel and the car pedals and brakes built into the car.
