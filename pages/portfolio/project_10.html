Wordwee.com

Wordwee.com is an educational website that promotes early learning from the ages of 2 to 6. During these critical first few years of life a child is like a sponge. Soaking in all kind of information. These years are the best time for learning. Wordwee.com is a game that teaches children word recognition. Combining education with entertainment. If your child matches the word correctly, they will get to watch an entertaining video. Through repetition, children will learn how to recognize words. This promotes early reading and makes your child a life time reader.
