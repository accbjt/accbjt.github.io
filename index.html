Me Myself and I

“Good things come to those who wait… greater things come to those who get off their ass and do anything to make it happen.”

  • github Twitter LinkedIn quora stackoverflow
    Bill John Tran
    Web Developer

    I am a natural builder. Kind of like Bob the builder but I am Bill the builder. I started out my career as an Automotive paint and body guy, because I was fascinated by the import car scene. I loved it so much that I wanted to build custom cars and design them. I went to school for art, but decided to go for the hands on route. I am definitely a hand on type of guy. I was so motivated to learn everything about making prototype models, that I went out and got as much hands on experience as I could. I am now capable of building and designing models from a drawing or from scratch. I also have experience in graphic design and a little bit of product design. Now that I am older my focus has changed to the digital age and I'm moving towards software development. I am currently enrolled in Coding House for training on Full Stack Web Development. I will be graduating on June 6, 2014. You can follow me on my blog to see how this adventure goes.
